# Photoshop

Photoshop is industry standard photo editing software, but it come at a cost.
If you can afford it, then go for it, but there's also a number of free & paid
alternatives at a fraction of the cost.

## Cost

Photoshop is a subscription-based software. It costs around £21.98 a month, but
also has a student plan that gives you access to all creative cloud apps for £16.24 a month.

Our costs may be out of date, please [check photoshop's](https://www.adobe.com/uk/products/photoshop/plans.html) website for more info.
