DaVinci Resolve is a professional video editing software developed by Blackmagic
Design. It's the industry standard for video editing, and is used by many
professionals in the industry.

### Cost

The free version of DaVinci Resolve is perfectly fine for developers, and the paid
version is usually not required. If needed there is a one-time purchase
requirement for the full version. The full version costs around $499 for a single
license, with discounts available for students and teachers.
