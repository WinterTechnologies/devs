Final Cut Pro is a professional video editing software for MacOS. It is used by
maby many professionals on youtube for example.

### Cost

Final Cut Pro is available through the MacOS App Store for a one-time purchase
of £299.99. It is also available as part of the Pro Apps Bundle for Education,
which includes Final Cut Pro, Logic Pro, Motion, Compressor, and MainStage, for
£199.99.
