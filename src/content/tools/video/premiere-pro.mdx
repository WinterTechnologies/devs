Premiere Pro is a professional video editing software developed by Adobe. It's
the industry standard for video editing, and is used by many professionals in the
industry.

### Cost

Premiere Pro is available through a standalone subscription, or as part of the
Creative Cloud package. The standalone subscription costs around £21.98/per momth
for a single license, with discounts available for students and teachers. The Creative
Cloud package costs around £56.98/per month, and includes all Creative Cloud
apps, including Premiere Pro.
